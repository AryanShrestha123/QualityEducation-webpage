Factors Affecting Quality Education

The first table highlights key factors that influence education quality worldwide.

Factor Description Impact
Teacher Training Quality of training and certifications for educators Highly trained teachers improve student outcomes
Infrastructure Availability of classrooms, libraries, and technology Better infrastructure leads to improved learning
Student-Teacher Ratio Number of students per teacher Lower ratios provide more individual attention
Government Funding Public investment in education More funding enhances resources and access
Parental Involvement Engagement of parents in children's education Increased involvement improves student motivation
Technology Integration Use of digital tools in classrooms Enhances engagement and access to information
